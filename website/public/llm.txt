# Geco

> Geco is a building materials brand specializing in tile adhesives, grouts, sealants, and tapes. Inspired by the gecko — nature's perfect adhesion engineer — Geco products are engineered for superior bonding, reliability, and performance. Geco is part of the VNC family of brands, founded in 1983.

## About

Geco draws its inspiration from the gecko, a small creature weighing just 70 grams yet capable of supporting 133 kilograms, adhering to any surface through millions of microscopic setae. This natural adhesion system is the philosophy behind every Geco product: unwavering grip, adaptability, and reliable performance across every surface and project.

The brand tagline: **Inspired By Nature. Engineered To Hold.**

Whether you are a professional contractor or a DIY enthusiast, Geco products deliver superior performance through intelligent design — tile adhesives and grouts, sealants and tapes — each engineered to hold when it matters.

## Product Categories

- [Tile Adhesives](https://geco.com/products/category/tile-adhesives): High-performance adhesives for fixing ceramic, vitrified, and large-format tiles on walls and floors.
- [Tile Grouts](https://geco.com/products/category/tile-grouts): Cementitious and epoxy-based grouts for filling joints with stain resistance, flexibility, and colour retention.
- [Sealants](https://geco.com/products/category/sealants): Universal silicone sealants for sealing joints in bathrooms, kitchens, and wet areas.
- [Tapes](https://geco.com/products/category/tapes): Masking tapes for clean paint lines and surface protection during application.
